Nov, 2023

A household survey to assess the impact of drinking saline water in the coastal region of Barguna, Barishal

I had privilege of attending this household survey in the coastal region of Barguna. As the project manager, I played a multifaceted role, ensuring the success of this impactful initiative.

This survey was a part of the project 'Aquifer Salinity Solution' funded by Unicef Bangladesh, Jago Foundation.

My responsibilities was the design of questionnaires, collaborating with mentors, mapping the study area, managing all volunteers, etc.

This experience has been immensely fulfilling, underscoring the power of collaboration and community-driven initiatives in effecting positive change. I look forward to leveraging these insights to support further endeavors aimed at empowering vulnerable communities and fostering sustainable development in coastal regions.

Mar, 2023

Assessment of impacts of drought on agricultural vegetation and production in the Barind Tract, Bangladesh

In collaboration with Professor Md. Abdullah Salman, I actively contributed to the research project focused on assessing the impacts of drought on agricultural vegetation and production in the Barind Tract, Bangladesh. This endeavor harnessed the combined power of satellite, meteorological, and household observations to garner comprehensive insights.

My primary responsibility in this project was the meticulous analysis of satellite data. This involved the calculation of various drought-related indices such as NDVI, SVI, TCI, VCI, and VHI. These indices played a crucial role in assessing the severity and extent of drought impacts on agricultural vegetation. Furthermore, I was tasked with the critical role of mapping these indices to create visual representations that provided actionable insights for the research team.

Jan, 2023

Groundwater Sampling from Satkhira Sadar and Syamnagar Upazila

This fieldwork is completed under the supervision of Sukhen Goswami, Assistant Professor in Geology and Mining at the University of Barishal. My work involved the comprehensive collection of samples from various deep tubewells, followed by rigorous testing and mapping. The research spanned every union in these two upazilas, providing a detailed understanding of the region's groundwater conditions. Through this experience, I witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by coastal communities due to water salinity. This project not only deepened my understanding of geology but also underscored the critical importance of addressing water salinity issues for the well-being of these communities.
