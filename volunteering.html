Volunteering Activities
2023

Zoom the Earth 1.0

"Zoom the Earth 1.0" was organized by Barishal University Geophysical Society (BUGS) and YouthMappers at Barishal University at Geology and Mining Department, University of Barishal. The event was held in celebration of World Water Day and was attended by all the teachers and students in our department.

As an organizing member, I helped to plan and execute the event, which featured a presentation competition focused on water conservation and management. Teams from different years participated in the competition, with three teams being selected as winners and awarded prizes. All participants received participant certificates. Overall, it was a successful event that helped to raise awareness about the importance of water conservation and management.

2023

Geo Show - 2023

I volunteered at an event called "Geo Show," organized by YourMapper University of Barishal, University of Barishal Geophysical Society, and the EAGE Student Chapter. This three-day event showcased three geology-related movies: "The Core," "Jurassic World," and "The Day After Tomorrow," aimed at familiarizing incoming students with geological concepts. The junior batch showed great interest and gained valuable insights from the screenings. On the final day, professors from the department joined, emphasizing the importance of using movies as educational tools for understanding geological topics. The experience not only contributed to the event's success but also deepened the writer's appreciation for the impact of visual media in education.

2023

Chemical Metrology Olympiad (BRICM) 2023

The Chemical Metrology Olympiad (BRICM) 2023, organized by the Bangladesh Reference Institute for Chemical Measurements (BRICM) in collaboration with the Ministry of Science and Technology, aimed to promote scientific excellence. The competition comprised three groups, catering to students from 8th to 12th grade and undergraduates nationwide. Registration was open until July 23, 2023, with the first round held on July 28, 2023. The Olympiad covered a spectrum of subjects including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Environmental Science, IT, and Psychological Sciences. Notably, BRICM waived registration fees, prioritizing accessibility. Questions were presented in both Bengali and English, ensuring inclusivity.

As a Barishal University Science Club volunteer, I played a crucial role in disseminating information about the event in the Barishal Division, engaging the local community in the pursuit of scientific knowledge. This involvement provided valuable experience in event promotion and community outreach, contributing to the success of the Olympiad in the Barishal Division. For more information, visit www.bricm.gov.bd.

2022

Summer Olympiad 2022

The BUSC Summer Olympiad - 2022 was a gathering of sharp minds at the University of Barishal, where students showed off their smarts in Science, Languages, and General Knowledge. The best performers were given special awards and certificates.

The most exciting part was the Prize Giving Ceremony at Jibananondo Hall. We had some important guests like Professor Dr. Md. Sadequl Arefin, Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Mohammad Badruzzaman Bhuiyan, Treasurer, and Dr. Md. Khorshed Alam, Proctor, University of Barishal. They made the ceremony even more special. The winners got fancy certificates and cool crests, and everyone who took part got certificates too.

I played a crucial role as an organizing member, making sure everything ran smoothly. We also gave certificates to our hardworking volunteers who made everything happen. This event was not just about winning, it was about everyone coming together to celebrate learning and doing their best. It was a day to remember!
